# multi-asset-risk-parity-engine-
An institutional grade risk parity allocator across 12 ETFs spanning equities, rates, credit, commodities, and gold. Implemented Euler risk decomposition, Ledoit-Wolf shrinkage covariance estimation, and Equal Risk Contribution (ERC) optimization via SLSQP. Benchmarked ERC against minimum-variance and equal-weight strategies over 15+ years
